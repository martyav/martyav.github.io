I'm Marty Hernandez Avedon. I'm a dev with a background in graphic design and writing.

I mostly work with iOS, but I do know some web, and am currently ramping up my skills in Java and Android.

I've written some programming tutorials and guides for beginners, which are listed under Articles. I also tutor adult learners in iOS/Swift, and am trying to organize a React Native study group.

If you need help on a mobile app, somebody to look over your cover letter, or design advice, feel free to get in touch with me. I love working with new coders and supporting other folks from "untraditional" backgrounds (whatever that may mean).

I also still do print work, so if you need somebody to design flyers, merch, or business cards for you, let me know! I'll be happy to help.
